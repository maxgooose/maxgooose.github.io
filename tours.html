Cultural Tours of Syria

Experience Syria's rich heritage through carefully curated tours led by trusted local guides who bring history and culture to life.

Discover Syria with Local Experts

Our guided tours offer an authentic, safe, and deeply meaningful way to experience Syria's incredible heritage. Led by trusted local guides and our foundation team, you'll discover the real Syria beyond the headlines—a land of ancient history, warm hospitality, and profound cultural depth.

Sacred Heritage Sites

Visit ancient synagogues, churches, and mosques that have stood for centuries, each telling stories of faith and coexistence.

Living Communities

Walk through historic neighborhoods where Muslims, Christians, and Jews have lived side by side for generations.

Rich History

Discover Damascus's legacy as a cradle of civilization and center of multicultural education and coexistence.

Why Choose Our Tours

  • Local Expertise: Our guides are native Syrians with deep knowledge of history, culture, and hidden gems.
  • Safety First: We prioritize your safety with carefully planned routes and local security coordination.
  • Small Groups: Intimate group sizes ensure personalized attention and authentic cultural exchanges.
  • Cultural Immersion: Meet local families, enjoy traditional meals, and experience genuine Syrian hospitality.

How to Book

Booking Process

  1. Contact Us: Reach out via WhatsApp, email, or phone to discuss your interests and dates.
  2. Customize Itinerary: We'll work with you to create the perfect tour based on your interests.
  3. Confirm Details: Review the final itinerary, pricing, and safety information.
  4. Secure Booking: Complete payment and receive confirmation with all tour details.

Important Information

  • Best Time to Visit: October to April offers the most comfortable weather.
  • Group Size: Maximum 8 people per group for personalized experience.
  • Requirements: Valid passport and visa required. We assist with visa process.
