ABOUT

For over seven years, the Syrian Mosaic Foundation has been a driving force in Syria—preserving cultural heritage, developing communities, and uniting people across borders through direct, sustained action on the ground.

02
OUR VALUES

Connect

We build lasting relationships between communities, institutions, and leaders—creating networks of trust that drive real progress in Syria.

Inspiration

Seven years of action have shown us what Syria needs—and we move with urgency, clarity, and purpose to deliver it.

Passion

Deep commitment to Syria's people, heritage, and future fuels everything we do—from cultural preservation to community empowerment.

Authenticity

Every initiative we lead is rooted in firsthand knowledge—years of living, working, and building in Syria give us insight that no outsider can replicate.
03
TEAM

Behind every initiative is a team with deep roots in Syria —leaders who have spent years on the ground, building the relationships and expertise that make our impact possible.

Hamid Jalal

Founder

I left behind a career in law to pursue what I believed Syria needed most: someone willing to show up, stay, and build. Over the past decade, I've spent years on the ground in Syria—not as a visitor, but as someone deeply embedded in its communities, its culture, and its future.

Fluent in English, Arabic, and French, I've built relationships with government officials, religious leaders, artisans, and families across the country. These connections are the backbone of everything the Syrian Mosaic Foundation does—from heritage restoration and artifact recovery to cultural exchange programs that bridge Syria and the world.

Our work goes far beyond travel. We lead heritage preservation projects, sponsor cultural performances at the Damascus Opera House, facilitate interfaith delegations, and invest in community development. When we do bring people to Syria, it's with purpose: to witness firsthand a nation's resilience and to become part of its story.

I also volunteer at a local orphanage—a reminder that our mission is ultimately about people, and that every initiative must serve the communities we work alongside.

This is not an organization built on ideas alone. It's built on years of action, presence, and results.

04
OUR SERVICES

With over a decade of experience and seven years of continuous presence in Syria, the Syrian Mosaic Foundation leads in heritage preservation, community development, and cross-cultural engagement. Our work spans artifact recovery, site restoration, educational initiatives, and cultural diplomacy—supported by a trusted network of local leaders, institutions, and international partners.

We approach every project with the same care we show to family. Whether we're restoring a centuries-old synagogue, coordinating an interfaith delegation, or welcoming visitors to experience Syria firsthand, we go above and beyond—because the stakes are real and the communities we serve deserve nothing less.
