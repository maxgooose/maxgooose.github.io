01 — Leadership

Our Founders

A Syrian-American Jew and a Syrian-American Muslim — childhood neighbors from Damascus, now building a foundation together in New York.

Founder

Joe Jajati

Founder

Syrian-American Jew from New York and Damascus. Inspired by his grandfather — a former leader of Syria's Jewish community — Joe founded SMF to preserve and celebrate Syria's mosaic of cultures, faiths, and traditions.

Joe@syrianmosaicfoundation.org

03 — On the Ground

Our Work in Damascus

From documenting sacred sites to building diplomatic bridges — our team is on the ground making it happen.

Preserving Sacred Heritage
Documenting and recording religious texts and artifacts from Syria's diverse communities.
Documenting Coexistence
The living mosaic of Damascus — where neighbors of different faiths share the same streets.
Diplomatic Engagement
Building international partnerships to support heritage preservation and cultural exchange.
Sacred Spaces
Protecting and celebrating Syria's ancient synagogues, mosques, and churches.
Establishing Ties
Forging connections between communities, institutions, and cultural leaders.
Community Dialogue
Ongoing conversations about heritage, identity, and the future of Syria's cultural landscape.
