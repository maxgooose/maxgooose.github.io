WHERE TO STAY

Morocco is full of beautiful surprises—hidden palaces, quiet alleys, and soulful, handcrafted décor. Paired with warm hospitality, every stay feels authentic and inviting. At IMA AICHA, we select unique places rich in character, not generic hotels. With our experience and local insight, we curate the perfect stays to match your journey.

Chic Riads

A riad is a traditional Moroccan courtyard house, plain outside but ornate inside. Many are now boutique hotels, modern or classic, big or small. Staying in one offers a unique taste of Morocco’s charm.

Convert Kasbahs

Morocco’s boutique stays shine in elegant Kasbahs — historic forts hidden in southern palm oases. Behind mud walls, they reveal Morocco’s rich past and timeless charm.

Fancy Glamping

Luxury desert camping in Morocco is magical. Private camps or established sites let you sleep under stars, gather by campfires, and enjoy warm local hospitality from sunset to sunrise.

Discover Morocco

4 Imperial Cities

Morocco is home to four unique imperial cities - Marrakech, Fes, Rabat, and Meknes. These cities were once the capitals of different dynasties in Moroccan history and are known for their rich cultural and historical heritage.

4 Mountain Ranges

Morocco is home to four major mountain ranges: the Atlas Mountains, Rif Mountains, Anti-Atlas Mountains, and the Middle Atlas Mountains.

Over 300 Days of Sunshine

Many regions of Morocco enjoy over 300 days of sunshine a year, making it a year-round destination for travelers seeking warmth and clear skies.
