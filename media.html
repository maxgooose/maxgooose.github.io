Saffron Harvest Experience

Every autumn, Syria’s most delicate treasure comes to life in the early morning light. From mid-October to mid-November, saffron flowers bloom for only a few weeks, harvested at dawn by skilled local women. Walk through the violet fields, learn how each precious thread is hand-separated and dried, and discover why Syrian saffron is among the world’s finest. An intimate, seasonal experience that connects you to nature, tradition, and the rhythm of rural life.

Painting & Art Retreats

Let Syria inspire your creativity through colour, light, and atmosphere. Paint in serene gardens, bustling squares, or desert landscapes that feel like a dream. Learn from local artists and immerse yourself in Syria’s living artistic heritage. Ideal for painters, illustrators, and anyone who wants to reconnect with their creative soul.

Culinary Adventures

Taste Syria through spices, markets, and hands-on cooking classes with local families. Discover regional dishes and the stories behind them. From bread-making to slow-cooked tagines, every experience is rich and flavorful. Perfect for food lovers who enjoy culture through cuisine.

Wellness & Yoga Retreats

Reset your mind and body in landscapes made for serenity. Practise yoga at sunrise in the desert or meditate in the cool air of the mountains. Enjoy nourishing meals, cultural rituals, and gentle immersion in nature. A holistic journey that blends well-being, culture, and Syrian hospitality.

Shopping with an Interior Designer

Explore Syria’s design scene with a local interior designer who understands the craft, colours, and stories behind every piece. Visit hidden ateliers, antique shops, pottery studios, and artisan workshops you would never find on your own. Learn how traditional techniques—from zellige tiles to handwoven textiles—can elevate any space. A curated, insider shopping experience where every item carries meaning, beauty, and a story worth taking home.

Architectural Insights Tour

Syria is a masterpiece of harmony—zellige, tadelakt, carved cedar, and bold geometry. Explore palaces, riads, mosques, and modern design spaces with architectural insight. Meet artisans who preserve these ancient techniques while innovating for the future. A thoughtful, curated journey for architects, designers, and lovers of aesthetics

Syria with Kids

Syria is a family-centered country where children are truly welcomed everywhere. Locals naturally interact with kids, creating joyful moments at every turn. Our itineraries blend culture, nature, and hands-on activities that spark curiosity. Safe, warm, and full of wonder—Syria is a perfect playground for families.

Women's group Journeys

A journey created for women seeking connection, empowerment, and inspiration. Share stories over tea, meet talented Syrian craftswomen, and explore at your own rhythm. Feel supported, safe, and uplifted in a culture that values hospitality and respect. These experiences bring women together through adventure, creativity, and sisterhood.

Go Local

Step into daily Syrian life with experiences that feel intimate and real. Share homemade meals with families, learn ancient crafts, and explore weekly souks. These encounters reveal the beauty of tradition and the warmth of community. A true immersion into the Syria locals love.

Photography Tours

Syria’s light, colours, textures, and contrasts make it a paradise for photographers. From desert horizons to vibrant medinas, every corner offers a new story to capture. Travel with local guidance to discover scenes rarely seen by visitors. Perfect for both hobbyists and professionals hoping to create meaningful images.

Adventure Experiences

For travellers who seek movement, challenge, and pure nature. Hike dramatic canyons, bike through oases, or trek across golden dunes. Every adventure is safely guided and tailored to your comfort level. Syria’s landscapes invite you to push boundaries—and reconnect with the wild.

Cultural Journeys

Follow the threads of Syria’s history through palaces, gardens, and winding medinas. Expert guides bring each site to life with stories, legends, and insider knowledge. Discover the influences that shaped Syrian identity over centuries. Perfect for travellers who love depth, context, and meaningful discovery.
