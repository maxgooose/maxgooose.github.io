OUR PARTNERS

Syria is full of passionate people—dedicated artisans, local guides, and community leaders who preserve their heritage with pride. At Syrian Mosaic Foundation, we partner with trusted organizations and individuals who share our vision of cultural preservation and community empowerment.

Local Artisan Cooperatives

We work with skilled craftsmen preserving traditional Syrian arts—from mosaic work to textile weaving. Every partnership helps sustain ancient techniques for future generations.

Cultural Institutions

Collaborating with museums, universities, and cultural centers to document, preserve, and share Syria's rich heritage with the world.

Community Leaders

Our network of local guides and community leaders ensures authentic experiences while supporting grassroots initiatives across Syria.

SYRIA
BY NUMBERS
Discover Syria

6 UNESCO World Heritage Sites

Syria is home to six UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Ancient City of Damascus, Palmyra, Aleppo, Bosra, and Crac des Chevaliers—testaments to millennia of civilization.

10,000+ Years of History

Damascus is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, with evidence of settlement dating back over 10,000 years.

3 Major Religions' Holy Sites

Syria holds sacred sites for Christianity, Islam, and Judaism—including the Umayyad Mosque, Chapel of Ananias, and ancient synagogues of Damascus.
