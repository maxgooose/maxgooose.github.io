We craft immersive, luxurious journeys that reveal Morocco's hidden soul—its people, traditions, and untold stories. Where mystery lingers behind closed doors, we hold the key to unforgettable experiences.

Hamid Jalal

Founder

What do you do when you’re expected to wear a lawyer’s suit, but your heart beats for the open desert and the winding alleys of ancient cities? For me, the answer was simple: I traded in legal textbooks for breathtaking landscapes and found my true calling as a guide.

With over 10 years of guiding experience, I’ve led countless travelers through Morocco’s most stunning corners, from the majestic Atlas Mountains to the golden dunes of the Sahara. Fluent in English, French, and Tamazight, I bring stories to life, blending rich cultural knowledge with humor and energy.

At IMA Aicha Moroccan Adventures, we don’t just offer tours—we offer an escape into Morocco’s soul, tailored to perfection. We specialize in luxury, private experiences, ensuring every moment is crafted with care and elegance. Whether it’s a sunset camel ride in the desert, a private dinner under the stars, or exploring hidden gems with a local guide, I ensure your journey is nothing short of extraordinary.

Beyond the luxury travel experience, I am deeply passionate about giving back to my community, volunteering at a local orphanage. It’s a way for me to stay rooted in the values that make each of my tours a meaningful, authentic adventure.

Join us, and let’s create a Moroccan experience that’s as unique as you are.

OUR SERVICES

Ima Aicha Moroccan Adventures boasts over 10 years of experience in destination management and tourism, crafting unique travel experiences tailored to your preferences. Discover hidden gems and distinctive locations while embracing ethical travel practices. Our dedicated local guides ensure both your enjoyment and safety, offering insightful storytelling and a strong commitment to preserving cultural heritage.

We value family greatly and take great pride in looking after our visitors. Like a mother taking care of her family, we go above and beyond to accommodate your needs. This commitment is demonstrated by Ima Aicha herself, who happily makes couscous in the late evening, even if it takes her three hours. In a same vein, we make every effort to fulfill every request from visitors, no matter how much time or work it takes, in order to guarantee your pleasure.
