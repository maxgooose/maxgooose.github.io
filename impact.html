Our Impact

Building bridges, preserving heritage, and fostering unity across communities through tangible, on-the-ground action.

Initiative Highlights

Heritage Preservation

We are actively restoring and protecting historical sites of religious and cultural significance across Syria. Our focus includes Jewish heritage landmarks—synagogues that have stood for centuries—alongside sites important to all of Syria's diverse communities. Each restoration project is approached with care, authenticity, and respect for the site's historical and cultural significance.

Honoring Syria's Diverse Past

Syria's history as a cradle of civilization is reflected in its religious and cultural landmarks. From ancient synagogues to churches and mosques, each site tells a story of coexistence that spans millennia.

Historical records from the 1930s show Damascus as a thriving multicultural center, with Muslim, Christian, and Jewish students learning side by side—a tradition we work to honor and revive.

Delegations & Cultural Tours

Our guided tours and delegations bring together people from across the globe—academics, religious leaders, cultural enthusiasts, and families seeking to reconnect with their heritage. These experiences showcase Syria's warmth, hospitality, and cultural depth while providing meaningful exchanges between visitors and local communities.

Strategic Partnerships

We build influential partnerships primarily in the USA and Syria, working with organizations and individuals globally. By strategically engaging with influential people from all sides, we use our experience and knowledge to bridge divides and amplify our combined impact for Syria's renewal.

Through strategic alliances with cultural institutions, government entities, and community leaders, we create lasting impact that spans borders and brings together diverse perspectives in service of Syria's cultural renaissance.

Cultural Center in Damascus

We are establishing a vibrant hub in the heart of Damascus—a space for art, education, historical archives, and intercultural dialogue. This center will serve as a gathering place for locals and visitors alike, fostering unity and celebrating Syria's rich cultural mosaic.

Media & Educational Support

We produce media and documentation that tells Syria's powerful story of coexistence and resilience. Additionally, we provide modern educational resources to Syrian students, investing in the country's next generation and ensuring that young Syrians have the tools they need to build a brighter future.

Celebrating Cultural Excellence

The Syrian Mosaic Foundation is incredibly proud to sponsor the magnificent "وردة اشبيلية" at the Damascus Opera House.

In partnership with the Syrian Ministry of Culture, we are supporting one of the biggest dynamic presentations of Damascus's rich cultural art and incredible show skills. This powerful live performance features esteemed Syrian actors and dancers, celebrating the artistic heritage that defines Syria's cultural identity.

Our support reflects our belief that art and music are an integral part of ancient Syrian civilization, and we are committed to enhancing the local presence of musical theater through sustainable institutional cooperation.

A Legacy of Coexistence

These historical documents from 1936 reveal Damascus as a thriving center of multicultural education, where students of all faiths learned together.

The data shows thousands of Muslim, Christian, and Jewish students attending schools across Damascus—a testament to Syria's rich tradition of diversity and shared community. This legacy of peaceful coexistence is what we strive to honor and help restore through our work today.

Such records remind us that Syria's diversity is not a modern ideal but a historical reality, deeply woven into the fabric of Syrian society for centuries.
